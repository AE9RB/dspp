Detailed Description

Fast Fourier Transforms.

\[ X_k = \sum_{n=0}^{N-1} x_n e^{-{i 2\pi k \frac{n}{N}}} \qquad k = 0,\dots,N-1 \]

Performs a discrete Fourier transform on arrays with power-of-two sizes. Uses a radix-4 decimation in time algorithm with a time complexity of approximately \( \Theta(3/8N \log_2 (N)) \). Unlike most other FFT implementations, no planning step is necessary.

Example usage: 

std::array<std::complex<float>,512> data;
dspp::FFT::dft(data);

C-style arrays are supported: 

std::complex<float> data[512];
dspp::FFT::dft(data);

Out-of-place transform: 

std::array<std::complex<double>,64> in;
std::array<std::complex<double>,64> out;
dspp::FFT::dft(in, out);
